### Authorization

#### auth0

- **Type**: OAuth
- **Flow**: implicit
- **Authorization URL**: https://petstore3.swagger.io/oauth/authorize
- **Scopes**: 
  - write:pets: modify pets in your account
  - read:pets: read your pets
#### api_key

- **Type**: API key
- **API key parameter name**: api_key
- **Location**: HTTP header
